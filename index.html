About the event

Enlightenment 2020 is the annual science and technology festival of Smt. Indira Gandhi College of Engineering. It also refers to the independent body of Technical Committee who organize this event along with many other social initiatives and outreach programs around the year.

It is known for hosting a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures as well as workshops. The Vision for a new Decade is to enhance logical reasoning and develop a work culture among students.
